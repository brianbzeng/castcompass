machine-readable source of truth is
[`governance/authorship-provenance.json`](../governance/authorship-provenance.json); the live copy
is generated from [`app/data/structure-images.json`](../app/data/structure-images.json). CI checks
the source, creator, direct license link, change disclosure, and local SHA-256 for every row.
